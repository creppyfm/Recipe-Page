Fried Pickles

Description

If you've never experienced the absolute pleasure of biting down into the crunchy, yet tender, masterpiece that is a fried pickle, then I'm honestly excited for you. I remember my first time, and I'm so thankful for that experience. Fried pickles just might be the best damn food on the whole damn planet. And I am damn good at making them.

This recipe is simple (provided you have an air fryer,) and if followed correctly, will leave you with the best fried pickles you've ever tasted. The breading won't fall off. The pickles will be tender. The fat content will be minimal. Need I say more?

Ingredients

Here's what you'll need to get this done:

  • A solid Air Fryer
  • Olive Oil in a spary bottle
  • Sliced Pickles
  • Panko Breadcrumbs
  • Eggs
  • Water
  • Flour
  • Cajun Seasoning
  • Salt
  • Black Pepper

Steps

Let's get it done:

  1. Start by preheating your Air Fryer to 375 degrees.
  2. Place Flour in one bowl, Panko> in another, and Eggs(Beaten) in a third bowl.
  3. Add all spices to Flour, and only Salt, and Pepper to the Panko.
  4. Take Pickles and coat them evenly in Flour.
  5. Move Pickles to Egg mixture, making sure to coat them completely.
  6. Move the Pickles to the Panko mixture, again making sure to cover them completely.
  7. Lay the Pickles out on a pan, and spray with Olive Oil, flipping to coat both sides.
  8. Cook the breaded Pickles for about 6 minutes in your preheated Air Fryer.
  9. Finally, remove the Pickles, pair with a Garlic Aioli, and enjoy!
