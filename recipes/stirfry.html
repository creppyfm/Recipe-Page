Stir-Fry

Description

If the first recipe you clicked on was my world-famous Rice and Beans recipe, you're not in luck. In face, I'm sorry to say that this recipe is probably just as bad (if not worse,) and I will again strongly advise against actually attempting to recreate this monstrosity of a dish.

If you've just come from my moderately well-known Fried Pickles recipe, you're most-likely confused. How could such a fanstastic cook sink so low? Does an air fryer really make things that much easier? The answer to both questions is 19.

Ingredients

Moving on, here's what you'll need:

  • Veggies, all of them. Buy all of the veggies.
  • More veggies
  • Sesame seeds
  • Chicken
  • Stir-Fry Sauce
  • Some kind of oil. I don't think it's olive oil though. Oil != Olive Oil.
  • Mushrooms
  • The audacity

Steps

How dare you?

  1. Chop/skin/peel/shuck all of the veggies.
  2. Meanwhile, heat the not olive oil in a large sautee pan over medium very high heat.
  3. Add some or all of the veggies when the pan is hot.
  4. Add the rest of the veggies if you only added some.
  5. Cook for like five minutes or until the veggies stop pleading for their lives.
  6. Add Stir-Fry Sauce and turn the heat down to low.
  7. Damn, we forgot to make like rice or noodles to serve this over. Do that now.
  8. Damn, we also forgot to do anything with the chicken, so now this is Veggie Stir-Fry.
  9. Eat it if you want. I might try it just to get out of work the next couple of days. YMMV.
